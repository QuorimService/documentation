Badges in Quorim are visual indicators used to identify **official, trusted, or notable accounts** across the platform.

They help maintain **credibility and transparency** by clearly distinguishing authentic users and Quorim staff from regular accounts.
<Info>Badges are informational indicators only. They do not grant special permissions, authority, or rule exemptions.</Info> 

Badges may appear in:
- **Player Search**
- **Player profiles**
- **User information panels** across Quorim.

---

## Verified User

The **Verified User** badge is reserved for **notable and authentic individuals** within the Quorim ecosystem.

This badge confirms that an account belongs to a real, verified individual of significance or relevance to the Quorim community.

### Badge Assignment

| Aspect | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Authority** | Assigned **only by the Quorim Core Team** |
| **Selection Process** | Granted after internal review based on authenticity, notability, and community relevance |

### Badge Significance

- Confirms account authenticity
- Helps prevent impersonation
- Identifies notable individuals within the ecosystem

The Verified User badge **cannot be requested, purchased, or transferred**.

---

## Quorim Staff

The **Quorim Staff** badge identifies official members of the Quorim team.

The badge is **automatically removed** when a user is no longer part of the Quorim team.

<Danger>Any attempt to impersonate badge holders may result in flags or further action.</Danger>
