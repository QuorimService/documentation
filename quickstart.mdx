<Steps>

  <Step title="Login">
    [Sign in](https://quorim.xyz/login) using your Discord account to access your Quorim workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    After logging in, you’ll be redirected to the [<strong>Dashboard</strong>](https://quorim.xyz/dashboard), where all your servers and analytics live.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Server">
    Click on <strong>Create Server</strong> button and fill in the required details to set up your server.

    <Card
      title="Create Server"
      icon="server"
      iconType="duotone"
      href="/create-server"
      horizontal
    >
      Learn how to create a server, understand each field, and follow best practices.
    </Card>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finish Setup">
    Once your server is created:
    - It will appear in your dashboard  
    - Initial data syncing may take some time depending on server size
  </Step>

  <Step title="You’re Good to Go">
    After setup is complete, you can:
    - Invite <Tooltip headline="Invite Members" tip="Invite staff and team members to help manage and monitor your Quorim server." href="dashboard/settings#join-code"><strong>Members</strong></Tooltip> 
    - View <Tooltip headline="Analytics" tip="Deep insights into player activity, performance, combat, vehicles, and server trends in Quorim" href="/dashboard/analytics"><strong>Analytics</strong></Tooltip>  
    - Manage <Tooltip headline="Members" tip="View and manage members in your Quorim server" href="/dashboard/members"><strong>Members</strong></Tooltip>  
    - Review <Tooltip headline="Audit Logs" tip="Track important actions and changes made within your server" href="/dashboard/auditlogs"><strong>Audit Logs</strong></Tooltip>  
    - Configure <Tooltip headline="Settings" tip="Manage your server configuration, access, and ownership in Quorim"><strong>Settings</strong></Tooltip>  
  </Step>

</Steps>

<Check>
Analytics data will begin populating automatically once processing starts.
</Check>

<Info>
For large servers, analytics and historical data may take a short time to fully appear.
</Info>
